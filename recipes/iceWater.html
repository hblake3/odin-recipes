Ice Water

Sometimes I get back from a run, home from work, or take too long of a shower and I just get thirsty. In a pinch, this is what I do to quench my thirst.

Ingredients

  • 8 fl. oz. water
  • 7 standard U.S. ice cubes

Steps to Make It

  1. Take a bottle of water and place on counter.
  2. Get a glass, cup, bowl, Yahtzee Shaker, or any other liquid retaining container and place on counter next to the bottle of water.
  3. Fill up ice cube tray with water and place in freezer (wait 3 hours) (Note: if you have an ice cube dispenser, skip step 3).
  4. Pour water into container.
  5. WARNING: Stand back in case ice explodes.
  6. Wait 1 minute.
  7. Swirl with spoon.
  8. Find a comfortable couch, sip, and enjoy.
